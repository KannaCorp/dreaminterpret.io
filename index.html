Privacy Policy for Dream Interpreter & AI

This privacy policy describes the types of information that is collected and recorded by Dream Interpreter & AI and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or need more information about our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Consent

By using our application, you hereby agree to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

The personal information that you are asked for, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be clear to you at the time we ask you to provide your personal information.

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, the content of the message and/or attachment you may send us, and any other information you choose to provide.

AI Dream Interpretation (Google Gemini)

When you choose to use the AI dream interpretation feature, the dream description text that you enter is sent to Google's Gemini AI service in order to generate a personalized interpretation. We ask for your explicit consent before any text is sent.

The data sent is limited to the dream text you type. We do not send your name, email, location, or any device identifiers together with this request. The text is processed by Google to return an interpretation and is handled under Google's privacy policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not use this feature to collect or store your dream text on our own servers for any other purpose.

If you do not wish to share your dream text with the AI service, simply do not use the AI interpretation feature; the rest of the app works without it.

We use the information we collect in a variety of ways, including for:

  • Provision, operation and maintenance of our application
  • Improving, personalizing and expanding our application
  • Understanding and analyzing how you use our application
  • Developing new products, services, features and functionality
  • Finding and preventing fraud

Log Files

Dream Interpreter & AI follows a standard procedure of using log files. Information collected by log files may include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, device type, date and time stamp, and similar diagnostic data. Third-party ad providers such as Google may use device identifiers and advertising data to measure the effectiveness of advertising and to serve ads. Please note that Dream Interpreter & AI has no access to or control over the cookies or identifiers used by third-party advertisers.

Third Party Privacy Policies

Dream Interpreter & AI's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad providers for more detailed information.

Under the CCPA, California consumers have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected, to request deletion of their personal data, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. Each user is entitled to the following: the right to access, the right to rectification, the right to erasure, the right to restrict processing, the right to object to processing, and the right to data portability. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Data Retention, Deletion & Withdrawing Consent

We do not store your dream text on our own servers.
